How It Works

My Editing Process

A streamlined, transparent workflow designed to make collaboration effortless.

01

Submit Your Footage

Share your raw footage, assets, and project brief through our secure portal. We accept all major formats (MP4, MOV, MKV, etc.) via Google Drive, Dropbox, or WeTransfer.

Tip: Include references & mood boards for best results!
02

Project Discussion

We hop on a quick call or chat to align on your vision, style preferences, music choices, and specific requirements before a single cut is made.

Tip: Bring reference videos to speed up alignment!
03

Video Editing

Your footage goes through professional editing, color grading, sound design, motion graphics, and all the agreed-upon elements to create a polished masterpiece.

Tip: You'll get a rough cut preview before final delivery!
04

Review & Revisions

Watch the preview version in your private client portal, leave timestamped feedback, and we'll refine until it's exactly how you envisioned it.

Tip: Frame-level feedback ensures pixel-perfect revisions!
05

Final Delivery

Receive your final export in 4K/1080p with all required formats (YouTube, Instagram, TikTok). Files delivered via secure download link within 24 hours of approval.

Tip: Source project file available upon request!
